What is Speech & Language Therapy?

Speech and Language Pathology addresses communication disorders affecting speaking, understanding, reading, writing, and social skills. Our licensed specialists help children and adults overcome speech and language challenges with proven techniques.

From articulation to fluency, we use innovative approaches to help patients communicate effectively in all environments and build confidence in social interactions.

Advanced Techniques

Latest assessment and treatment methods proven effective

Family-Centered Care

Involving families actively in the therapy process for better outcomes

Conditions We Treat

Articulation Disorders

Difficulty pronouncing speech sounds correctly with targeted intervention

Voice Disorders

Changes in voice quality, hoarseness with specialized voice therapy

Language Delays

Slower development of language skills with personalized intervention

Stuttering/Stammering

Interruption in speech flow with fluency enhancement techniques

Cognitive-Communication

Difficulty with memory, attention with cognitive rehabilitation

Post-Stroke Communication

Recovery after neurological events with specialized rehabilitation
